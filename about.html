ABOUT ANML



This is Subliminal Activism
on a Global Scale





Whether the music has slipped into your consciousness behind hit TV shows (Fox’s Lucifer, The Royals, VICE, Wentworth, VICELAND, Reed Krakoff adds, or from her own visually stunning live multi-media concerts, ANML’s message in her latest album, WE.ANIMALS. is a potent reminder of how to live with, and alongside our "non-human animal” selves.

ANML’s latest show utilises interactive 3D mapped visuals projected onto a human-animal sized cage which surrounds her. The show leads the audience through a journey from embryo into confinement and ultimately freedom. Beautifully weaved together with ANML’s cinematic music, the audience is guided through the performance by ANML's heart felt interactions with the projections, the audience connects personally with her, themselves and to the underlaying message.
